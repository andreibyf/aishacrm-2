# Leads — User Guide

The AiSHA CRM **Leads** page helps you manage sales leads — the top of your sales funnel — before they become Opportunities, Accounts, or Contacts. This guide walks you through finding leads, filtering and sorting, changing the view, importing, converting, deleting, and working with test data.

## Finding leads

1. **Search** — use the search box at the top to find leads by name or company.
2. **Filters** — narrow the list using:
   - **Status** — filter by the lead's current stage (for example, leads that have been _converted_).
   - **Age** — defaults to "All Ages"; choose a range to focus on newer or older leads.
   - **Assigned To** — show only leads assigned to a particular person.
   - **Recently Updated** — focus on leads with recent activity.

## Filtering and sorting

1. **Tags** — select one or more tags to filter leads by tag.
2. **Sort** — order the list using any of:
   - Company A–Z / Company Z–A
   - Name A–Z / Name Z–A
   - Newest First / Oldest First
   - Recently Updated

## Changing the view

Switch between **grid**, **list**, and **card** views to display leads in the layout that works best for you.

## Importing leads

1. Click **Import leads from CSV**.
2. Select your CSV file to add leads in bulk.

## Converting a lead

1. Open (or select) the lead you want to convert.
2. Choose **Convert**, then pick the target: an **Opportunity**, **Account**, or **Contact**.
3. On success you'll see a "Lead converted successfully" confirmation, and the lead's status changes to **converted**.

## Deleting leads

1. Select one or more leads (use **Clear selection** to deselect).
2. Choose **Delete** and confirm in the dialog.
3. A confirmation appears: "Lead deleted successfully" (or "Failed to delete lead" if something goes wrong). Bulk delete is supported for multiple leads at once.

## Working with test data

Use the **Show Test Data** toggle to display sample leads for demos or training; it changes to **Showing Test Data** while active. Toggle it off to return to your real leads.

## Tips

- **Refreshing** — refresh the page to pull in new or updated leads; you'll see a "Leads refreshed" confirmation.
- **Role-based access** — some actions depend on your role (user, employee, manager, director, admin, or superadmin). If you can't see a feature, check your permissions with an administrator.
- **Start in the funnel** — leads are the entry point; qualify and convert them to move deals into Opportunities.
