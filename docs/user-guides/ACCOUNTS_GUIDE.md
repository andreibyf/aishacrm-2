# Accounts - User Guide

Manage your accounts efficiently with AiSHA CRM's Accounts page. This guide covers searching, sorting, creating, editing, deleting, importing, and managing related contacts for your business.

## Searching Accounts

1. **Search**: Enter keywords in the search bar to find specific accounts.
2. **Filters**:
   - **Type**: Filter accounts by their type.
   - **Assigned To**: Choose an employee to see their assigned accounts.
   - **Tags**: Filter by tags for quick access.

## Sorting Accounts

1. **Sort Options**:
   - **Name A-Z**
   - **Name Z-A**
   - **Newest First**
   - **Oldest First**
   - **Last Updated**

## Viewing Accounts

1. **Card View**: Displays a summary of each account with key details.
2. **List View**: Shows detailed information in a list format.

## Managing Accounts

### Creating an Account

1. Click the "New Account" button.
2. Fill in the required fields such as Name, Type, and Contact Information.
3. Assign the account to an employee if necessary.
4. Click "Save" to create the account. You'll see a confirmation message.

### Editing an Account

1. Select an account from the list or card view.
2. Click the "Edit" button next to the account name.
3. Make changes as needed and click "Save".

### Deleting an Account

1. Select the account you want to delete.
2. Click the "Delete" button.
3. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

## Importing Accounts

1. Click the "Import" button.
2. Choose a CSV file with your account data.
3. Follow the on-screen instructions to import the accounts.

## Refreshing and Bulk Actions

1. **Refresh**: Click the refresh icon to update the list of accounts.
2. **Select Multiple Accounts**:
   - Check boxes next to each account.
   - Use "Clear Selection" if needed.
3. **Bulk Delete**: After selecting multiple accounts, click "Delete". Confirm the deletion when prompted.

By following these steps, you can effectively manage your business accounts using AiSHA CRM's intuitive Accounts page.
