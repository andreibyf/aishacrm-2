# Communications - User Guide

The Communications page lets you manage email threads tied to your CRM records, with AiSHA's AI assistance for drafting replies. This guide covers viewing threads, replying, AI drafts, approvals, and linking messages to CRM records.

## Getting started

1. Select a tenant to view its communications ("Select a tenant to view communications").
2. The **Inbox** and **Threads** list load for that tenant.

## Finding and filtering threads

1. View all threads, or narrow by mailbox using **All mailboxes** or a specific **Mailbox**.
2. Open the **Filters** panel to refine the list. If nothing matches, you'll see "No threads matched these filters."
3. Click **Refresh** to pull in the latest messages.

## Reading a conversation

1. Click a thread to open its **Conversation**.
2. Threads are thread-aware, so replies keep the original "In-Reply-To" reference intact.

## Replying and composing

1. To respond, click **Reply** and fill in the **Reply Subject** and **Body**.
2. To start a new message, click **Compose Outbound Email** and enter the recipient, subject, and body.

## Using AiSHA's AI Reply Draft

1. In **Draft Instructions**, tell AiSHA how you'd like to respond to the latest message.
2. AiSHA generates an **AI Reply Draft** based on your instructions, which you can review and send.

## Approvals and queued activity

1. Some replies or actions may **Require Approval** and show "Review required."
2. Use **Review queued activity** to approve or manage pending items.
3. Use **Replay queued** to re-run items waiting in the queue.

## Linking messages to CRM records

1. Use the **Linked Entity** field (with an optional entity ID/UUID) to attach a thread to a specific CRM record.
2. Incoming messages can also flow into the **Lead Capture Queue** to be turned into leads.

## Tracking status

- Each message shows a **Delivery State** so you can see whether it sent.
- The **Recent Activity** panel highlights the latest actions on your conversations.
