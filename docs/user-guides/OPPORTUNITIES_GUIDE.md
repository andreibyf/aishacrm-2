# Opportunities - User Guide

The Opportunities page in AiSHA CRM helps you manage your sales deals as they move through your pipeline.

## Searching Opportunities

1. Click on the search bar at the top of the page to filter opportunities by stage, assigned to a specific employee, or tags.
2. Enter your criteria and press Enter to apply the filters.

## Sorting Options

1. To sort the list, click on any of the following options in the header row:
   - **Name A-Z** for alphabetical order
   - **Name Z-A** for reverse alphabetical order
   - **Newest First** for the most recent opportunities first
   - **Oldest First** for the oldest opportunities first
   - **Recently Updated** to see the most recently updated opportunities first
   - **Oldest Updated** to view opportunities based on when they were last updated

## Changing Opportunity Stages

1. Switch to the Kanban board view by clicking on "Switch view" at the top of the page.
2. Drag an opportunity card from one stage column to another to change its stage.

## Viewing Opportunities

1. Click on any opportunity card in either the Kanban or Table view to open it and see details such as:
   - Account
   - Contact
   - Close Date
   - Value (amount)
   - Stage
   - Linked Lead
   - Assigned Employee

## Linking Opportunities to Leads and Assigning Employees

1. When viewing an opportunity, click on the "Edit" button.
2. In the edit form, you can link the opportunity to a lead and assign it to an employee.

## Importing Opportunities from CSV

1. Click on the "Import" button at the top of the page.
2. Select your CSV file and upload it.

## Deleting Opportunities

1. To delete multiple opportunities:
   - Select them by clicking on the checkboxes next to each opportunity.
   - Click on "Clear selection" when you're done selecting.
   - Click on the "Delete" button at the top of the page.
2. To delete a single opportunity, click on the trash icon next to the opportunity.

## Refreshing the List

1. If you need to refresh the list of opportunities, click on the "Refresh" button at the top of the page.

These steps should help you effectively manage your sales opportunities in AiSHA CRM.
