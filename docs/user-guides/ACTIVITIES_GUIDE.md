# Activities - User Guide

The Activities page in AiSHA CRM helps you track all your tasks and interactions with clients, contacts, and potential leads efficiently.

## Creating an Activity

1. Click on the "+ New" button at the top of the Activities list.
2. Enter a **Subject** for the activity.
3. Set a **Due Date** if applicable.
4. Select the type of entity you want to link this activity to (Account, Contact, Lead, Opportunity, or Potential Lead).
5. Click "Save" to create the activity.

## Editing an Activity

1. Find and click on the activity you want to edit in the list.
2. Make your changes to the **Subject** or **Due Date** as needed.
3. Click "Save" to update the activity.

## Deleting an Activity

1. Find the activity you want to delete.
2. Click on the trash icon next to it.
3. Confirm by clicking "Delete" in the confirmation dialog.

## Searching and Filtering Activities

1. Use the search bar at the top of the list to find specific activities.
2. Apply filters:
   - **Status**: Filter activities by their status.
   - **Assigned To**: Choose the user who is assigned to the activity.
   - **Tags**: Filter by tags associated with the activities.

## Sorting Activities

Choose a sort order from the sort options:

- **Newest First**
- **Recently Updated**
- **Subject A-Z**
- **Subject Z-A**

## Importing Activities from CSV

1. Click "Import" at the top of the list.
2. Choose your CSV file and upload it.
3. Review any errors or warnings and make necessary adjustments.
4. Click "Submit" to import the activities.

## Refreshing the Activity List

1. Click on the refresh icon (usually a circular arrow) at the top of the list.
2. The list will update with the latest data from your CRM.

## Selecting Multiple Activities for Bulk Deletion

1. Check the box next to each activity you want to delete.
2. Click "Clear selection" if needed.
3. Click on the trash icon and then "Delete Selected" in the confirmation dialog.

By following these steps, you can efficiently manage all your activities within AiSHA CRM.
