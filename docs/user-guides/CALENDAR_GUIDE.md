# Calendar - User Guide

The Calendar page in AiSHA CRM is a visual timeline of your CRM activities — tasks, meetings, and calls. Use it to see your schedule at a glance and to connect an external calendar.

## Navigating the calendar

1. Use the toolbar to switch between **Day**, **Week**, and **Month** views.
2. Click **Today** to jump back to the current date.
3. Use the toolbar's navigation controls to move forward or backward through dates.

## Viewing activity details

1. Click any item on the calendar (a task, meeting, or call) to open the **Activity Detail** panel.
2. The panel shows the activity's information; calendar items are the same records you'll find in the **Activities** module, so changes stay in sync.

## Connecting an external calendar

AiSHA CRM can sync with an outside calendar so your events appear alongside your CRM activities.

1. Open **Manage External Calendar Integration** (also available from the **Integrations** page).
2. Connect your provider — **Google** and **Outlook** calendars are supported.
3. The calendar shows a **Connected** or **Not Connected** status so you can confirm the link is active.

## Tips

- Switch to **Week** or **Day** view when you need to see time slots in detail; use **Month** for a broad overview.
- Activities are color-coded by type, making it easier to tell tasks, meetings, and calls apart at a glance.
- Keep your external calendar connected so meetings booked elsewhere stay visible in AiSHA CRM.
